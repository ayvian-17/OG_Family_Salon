Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Welcome to OG Family Salon. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, how we process it, and the safeguards we put in place to ensure your booking data remains secure.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal identifiers that you voluntarily share with us when requesting bookings or reviews on our website, including:

  • Your full name and contact coordinates (phone number).
  • Requested services, date, time, and optional customized salon request details.
  • Browser cookies, operational analytics, IP addresses, and session duration data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly to deliver the ultimate salon hospitality, specifically to:

  • Coordinate booking schedules and verify appointments directly.
  • Send reminders, confirmation updates, or notify you of schedule adjustments.
  • Refine our visual styling layouts and improve user experience audits.
  • Respond to client support requests or moderating review entries.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not lease, trade, or distribute your private coordinate details to third-party advertising companies. Your details may only be shared under the following conditions:

  • To comply with regulatory laws or legal directives.
  • With hosting infrastructure networks and verification partners that help operate this portal under strict confidentiality contracts.

4. Data Security

We enforce premium industry-standard cybersecurity measures to protect your client form bookings. However, please note that no system of transmission over the web is completely invulnerable to security breaches.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website utilizes cookies to save your custom interface theme configurations (dark/light mode setting). You can instruct your browser to block all cookies, though this may prevent the platform from loading your preferred style parameters automatically.
